The Impossible Triangle — India's Three Crude Options

There is no clean exit from this bind. Each option carries a distinct, painful trade-off India must navigate simultaneously.

🇷🇺
Russian Crude Geopolitical Risk
✅ Discounted pricing, established logistics, 5–7 day transit
⚠️ US tariff threat if imports resume at scale. India cut Russian purchases from 1.71M to 1.16M bpd in 2025 under pressure
→ Available but politically toxic
🌍
Spot Market Crude Fiscal Risk
✅ No geopolitical strings attached, diversified sources
⚠️ At $84+ and rising, every $10 bbl rise costs India $20 billion more annually. Extended closure could push Brent to $120–150 (Bernstein extreme case)
→ Expensive and escalating
📉
Fiscal Deficit Expansion Market Risk
✅ Absorb subsidy shock through government books short-term
⚠️ Fiscal math breaks at $100+; rating agencies watching. CAD widens 50 bps per $10 rise. Bond yields spike, INR weakens, RBI hamstrung
→ Buys time but worsens outlook
